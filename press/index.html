Description:

Mixolumia introduces a fresh twist on falling block puzzlers. Scintillating visuals and entrancing music respond to your input and evolve as you progress while diamond blocks slide together on a gem-shaped board and explode in glittering chain reactions.

Mixolumia comes with music by davemakes, Josie Brechner, and Jasmine Cooper. Each track has unique sound effects that harmonize with the music, giving the game a totally different feel depending on which song you choose.

Musicians can even create and/or mod their own songs and sound effects for Mixolumia. Check out the dynamic music system documentation to learn more. Check out the many community-made soundpacks on Itch.io and in the Discord.

Features:

  • Arcade style gameplay
  • Dynamic, evolving music
  • 6 game modes: Marathon, Endless, Rush, Intense, Relaxed, and the secret Impossible mode
  • 50+ color palettes
  • Players can create their own color palettes
  • Players can create their own music and sound effects
  • Accessibility options to disable effects

Dave Hoffman AKA davemakes (they/them) is an American indie game developer living in Chiba, Japan. They created the Playdate game Executive Golf DX, and they have contributed art to games like EXTREME MEATPUNKS FOREVER and An Airport for Aliens Currently Run by Dogs.
